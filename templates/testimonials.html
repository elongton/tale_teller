Enrique Longton

I worked with Tellers of Tales after writing a series of short stories. The developmental editing service was just the kind of analysis my stories needed. Laurel gives feedback that takes the form of a discussion, not just some one-sided information spout. I found it helpful because her questions brought items to light that I hadn’t considered. For example, one of my characters made an appearance in the beginning of a story and did not reappear until the very end. Laurel assured that with the events that occurred between the character’s first and second appearance in the story, that his behavior was consistent with how I originally imagined him.

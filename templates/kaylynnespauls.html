About Kay

"I am the Teller of Tales."

I write fantasy for Adults and Children. I live in Raleigh NC, with my wonderful husband and I'm hard at work on the rest of original series'! I also love to read, play video games, cook, watch movies, go for walks, and obviously write.

I have worked with children since I was 16 and I absolutely love it. I have three and soon to be four nephews that I adore. I also work every summer at a Harry Potter themed girl-scout camp that is one of the most rewarding experiences I've ever had each time I go.
